Neo Banking for Bharat MSMEs

"OnePesa” is a Neo Banking solution for Bharat MSMEs to avail unique embedded financial services like savings, credit & government welfare schemes through a single channel

OnePesa Providing Best Services

At OnePesa, we aspire to provide easy & affordable financial products and services to our customers & cater to the ever growing under-served markets & geographies.

Financial Wellness Solution

OnePesa’s offers offers OnePesa offers PAN, ITR & GST enrolment as well as filling services at an affordable price.

Government Welfare Schemes

OnePesa facilitates & educate MSMEs to government welfare schemes through its partners.

Banking

MSMEs can avail all type of assisted banking services like deposit & withdrawal through OnePesa merchant points.

Savings

OnePesa savings is a long/medium & short term goal based savings solutions that is recommended to MSMEs and families who have the habit of periodic savings.

Credit

OnePesa offers lending services to MSMES through its lending partner who uses AI and big data technology to help underwrite customer & offer customised lending solutions.

Micro Pension

OnePesa has partnered with micro pension service provider to offer a unique pension product which helps Bharat MSMEs & families to save for old age while young.

Access To Finance

OnePesa promotes economic growth and enhancing livelihoods through deepening financial inclusion & resilience for Bharat MSME target group.

OnePesa for Business

OnePesa for Business is a distribution ecosystem to support the Bharat MSMEs at the last mile through assisted B2B2C channel.

Protect your money

Our bank has been providing services to its customers for almost 25 years.
